About Us!

At Sweet Bakery, we believe life's too short to skip dessert. What started as a cozy kitchen dream has grown into your local go to for feel good, made from-scratch baked goods. We’re a small, heartwarming bakery built on big love for butter, sugar, and the simple joy a warm cookie can bring.
Every treat we make from our signature cupcakes and melt in your mouth brownies to hand rolled pastries and flaky croissants is made with the same care and quality we’d bake for our own family. We use real ingredients, time tested recipes, and a sprinkle of creativity in every batch.
Whether you're grabbing your morning muffin, ordering a cake for a celebration, or just need a sweet pick me up, we're here to make your day a little brighter and a lot tastier.
Thanks for letting us be a small part of your sweetest moments.

With love & sugar
The Sweet Bakery Team

Best home made brownies recipe ingredients


Ingredients

2 eggs + water We use a bit of water with eggs to achieve the moist, gooey texture. Just beacause this recipe doesn't contain any baking powder, eggs are essential for helping the brownies puff up in the oven.
Powdered sugar Powdered sugar contains cornstarch which helps thicken the batter.
Unsweetened cocoa powder Make sure to sift it if it's lumpy!
Oil I use olive oil because it's what I keep on hand for the rich flavor
Vanilla Extract 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla really enhances the richness of the chocolate and brings out its deep flavor.


Step 1: Mix together the dry and wet ingredients in two separate bowls. Combine the sugar, flour, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, chocolate chips if desired, bit of salt in a medium bowl.
Step 2: Whisk together the eggs, olive oil and water in a large bowl.
Step 3: Combine the wet and dry ingredients. sprinkle the dry mixture over the wet one and fold it until just combined.
Step 4: Pour the batter into 8x8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper. Use a spatula to spread it to all four sides of the pan and to smooth the top.
Step 5: Bake at 325 degree oven for 40 to 45 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out with a few crumbs attached.

Allow the brownies to cool completely before slicing. Enjoy!
